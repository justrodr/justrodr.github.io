AC Angel

This is an idea I've worked on several times with different teams and solo. It's an AC filter subscription service for private landlords. One of the biggest costs private landlords who invest in residential real estate can incur is fixing a broken AC, and there's an easy preventative solution: change the air filters regularly. The problem is that tenants are not incentivized to change the filters themselves. AC Angel aimed to bridge this gap by first developing a way for homeowners to regularly deliver AC filters to each of their properties, and to provide instructions and rewards for tenants.
