    Winning 2nd Place at Texas A&M's premier all-engineering hackathon

    I had the privilege of competing in two Aggies Invent Competitions. Aggies Invent is like an entrepreneurial engineering hackathon at Texas A&M University. In my second time competing, my team brought innovation to the construction industry by developing a concrete formula that utilizes recycled plastic. We came up with a business plan and sales pitch, and we presented our ideas to a panel of industry experts. My role in the team was to conduct market research, and develop all of the presentation materials (charts, graphs, presentation slides, video, etc...).
