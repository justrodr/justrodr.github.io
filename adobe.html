A Week At Adobe

Through rigorous networking and incredible luck, I was able to land an externship at Adobe in 2015. I had emailed all of my parents' friends, my firends' parents, and the faculty and staff asking to send me contacts that they might have of people who work in Silicon Valley. At this time I already had a strong desire to pursue a career in tech, and I was determined to create learning opportunities. When I finally got in contact with an employee at Adobe and asked about opportunities that they might know of, one of Adobe's executive's son was also looking to do an externship at Adobe. Serendipitously, I was able to join him for his externship. We learned about several different departments in Adobe, and got a true sense of what working at a large tech company would be like. I learned how I could create opportunities through hustle and grit.
