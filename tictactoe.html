Tic Tac Toe & Swift

In my freetime at school, I teach myself Swift and mobile development. I don't own a mac computer which is necessary to code in Swift, so I spend extra hours on campus using one of the two mac computers hidden in the back of one of the campus computer labs. I made a simple Tic Tac Toe game because I was inspired to help people interact in medial times when they would normally just recluse themselves by doing something menial on their phone. I learned how difficult it is to publish an app on the App Store (especially when you don't own the mac). I was driven to go through the entire process of getting the app published for the pride of being able to say that I have an app on the App Store. I am proud of how the game turned out, and I am looking to publish more apps in the near future.
